Welcome to Palembang

Palembang is the capital city of the South Sumatra province in Indonesia. The city is located on the banks of the Musi River and has a population of over 1.7 million people. Palembang is known for its rich history, culture, and cuisine.

Palembang Icons

Ampera Bridge

The Ampera Bridge is a landmark in Palembang and is one of the most famous bridges in Indonesia. The bridge spans the Musi River and connects the two parts of the city.

Pempek

Pempek is a traditional dish from Palembang and is made from fish and tapioca flour. The dish is usually served with a sweet and sour sauce and is a popular street food in the city.

Musi River

The Musi River is the longest river in South Sumatra and runs through the city of Palembang. The river is an important waterway for the city and is used for transportation and fishing.

Short History

Palembang has a rich history that dates back to the 7th century. The city was once the capital of the powerful Srivijaya Empire, which was one of the largest and most influential empires in Southeast Asia. The empire controlled much of the region's trade and was a center of Buddhist learning and culture.

The city was also an important port and trading center, and was known for its gold, spices, and textiles. Palembang was a cosmopolitan city with a diverse population of traders, sailors, and scholars from around the world.

Today, Palembang is a modern city that is known for its traditional culture, cuisine, and festivals. The city is a popular destination for tourists who come to explore its history, temples, and natural beauty.

Visit Us

Palembang is a vibrant city with a rich history and culture. The city is known for its traditional cuisine, festivals, and landmarks. Visitors to Palembang can explore the city's temples, museums, and markets, or take a boat ride on the Musi River.

Some of the city's most popular attractions include the Ampera Bridge, the Pempek Museum, and the Great Mosque of Palembang. The city is also home to a number of parks, gardens, and shopping centers where visitors can relax and enjoy the local culture.

Palembang is a city that offers something for everyone, whether you are interested in history, culture, or nature. So come and visit us and experience the beauty and charm of Palembang for yourself.
